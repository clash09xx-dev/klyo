Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Klyo ("we", "our", "us") is a CRM application for small businesses, accessible at klyolabs.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email address, and hashed password when you sign up.
  • Business data: contacts, companies, quotes, offers, notes, and activity logs you create inside the app.
  • Google account data (optional): if you connect your Gmail account to send emails from Klyo, we store a Gmail refresh token to send emails on your behalf. We access only the gmail.send scope — we never read your inbox or any other Gmail data. If you use "Sign in with Google", we receive your name and email address from Google.
  • Payment data: Stripe processes your payment information. We store your Stripe customer and subscription IDs but never your card number.
  • Usage data: anonymous page-view counts for our own internal analytics.

2. How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the Klyo CRM service.
  • To send transactional emails (email verification, billing receipts) when necessary.
  • To send business emails on your behalf via Gmail, if you have connected your Gmail account.
  • To enforce billing and tier limits.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not use your data to train AI models.

3. Gmail API usage — Limited Use policy

Klyo's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically: we only request the gmail.send scope. We use this access exclusively to send emails that you explicitly compose and trigger within Klyo. We do not read, store, or share the contents of your Gmail inbox or any other Gmail data.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway in the EU/US region. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authenticated requests using JWT tokens.

5. Data sharing

We share data only with the following third parties, as necessary to operate the service:

  • Railway — hosting and database infrastructure.
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Google — Gmail sending and Google sign-in (if you enable these).
  • OpenAI — AI email drafting (contact name and your instructions only; no full contact record is sent).

6. Data retention and deletion

You can delete your workspace and all associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@klyolabs.com. We will delete all your data within 30 days of your request.

Gmail refresh tokens are deleted immediately if you disconnect Gmail from Settings.

7. Cookies

Klyo uses localStorage in the browser to store your authentication token and theme preference. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@klyolabs.com.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of Klyo after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@klyolabs.com.
