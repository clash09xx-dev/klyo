Google integration

Why Klyo requests Gmail access

Klyo can optionally connect to your Gmail account to send business emails and track communication history with your clients. Here is exactly what we do — and what we don't.

Gmail access is used only to:
Connect your Gmail so Klyo can send emails on your behalf.
Send business emails — only messages you compose and approve before they go out.
Show communication history with your contacts to improve your CRM workflow.
We request only the gmail.send scope — write-only, no inbox access. Gmail data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train AI models. Disconnect anytime from Settings.
