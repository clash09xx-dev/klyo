Google integration

Why Klyo requests Gmail access

Klyo can optionally connect to your Gmail account. Here is exactly what that means and what we do with access.

Gmail access is used only to:
  • Connect your Gmail account so Klyo can send emails on your behalf.
  • Send business emails from inside Klyo — only messages you compose and explicitly approve before sending.
  • Show communication history with your contacts to improve your CRM workflow.

We never read your inbox. Klyo requests only the gmail.send scope — write-only access to send emails you approve. Gmail data is never sold, never shared with advertisers, and never used to train AI models. You can disconnect Gmail at any time from your Settings page.
